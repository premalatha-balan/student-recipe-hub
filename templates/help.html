📖 How to Use Student Grub Hub

🔍 Finding Recipes (The Pantry Search)

  1. Type ingredients in the search box (e.g., "Egg", "Pasta", "Cheese")
  2. Press Enter or click the "+" button to add them as tags
  3. Add all ingredients you have in your kitchen
  4. Click "🔍 Find Recipes"
  5. We'll show you only recipes that use ALL of those ingredients
💡 Pro Tip: Don't worry if you don't have everything - just add what you have and we'll find what you can make!

📝 Adding a New Recipe

  1. Click the "➕ Add Recipe" button in the top-right corner
  2. Fill in the recipe details:
    • Name - What's the dish called?
    • Description - A brief, appetizing summary
    • Cooking Time - How many minutes does it take?
    • Cost - Choose Cheap, Mid, or Treat
    • Instructions - Write step-by-step instructions
    • Image URL - Optional: link to a picture
  3. Add ingredients one at a time:
    • Type ingredient name (e.g., "Chicken")
    • Type quantity (e.g., "200g" or "2 breasts")
    • Click "+ Add" or press Enter
    • Repeat until all ingredients are listed
  4. Click "✅ Submit Recipe"
  5. Your recipe will be immediately available for everyone to find!
💡 Important: Ingredient names are automatically capitalized. If you add "egg" it becomes "Egg" - this helps with searching!

👀 Viewing Full Recipe Details

  1. On the home page, click any recipe card
  2. A popup will show you:
    • Complete ingredient list with quantities
    • Full step-by-step instructions
    • Cooking time and cost level
  3. Click the "✕" button or click outside the popup to close it

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if a recipe is cheap?

Each recipe shows a cost badge: 💰 Cheap - under £3 per portion, 💰💰 Mid - £3-£6 per portion, 💰💰💰 Treat - over £6 per portion

What if I want to add a recipe but don't have an image?

No problem! Just leave the image URL field empty. We'll automatically show a placeholder image.

Can I edit or delete a recipe after adding it?

Not yet! We're working on adding edit/delete features soon. For now, if you made a mistake, just add a corrected version.

Why can't I find my recipe when I search?

Make sure you're searching with ALL the ingredients in your recipe. The search only shows recipes that use every single ingredient you type in.
